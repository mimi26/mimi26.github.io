Hi! I'm Mimi.

I live in Brooklyn, NY with my cat Mutty. I'm a fire performer; I spin poi and fire fans, and recently started eating fire. I like to knit, and I dabble in aerial silks. I sing and play guitar, and occasionally I like to ferment things. I have 6 nieces and nephews, and greatly enjoy playing the role of crazy aunt. Burning Man has been a big part of my life since 2011. I worked as an Ophthalmic Photographer for the last 10 years. Now I'm excited to start a whole journey as a Web Developer.
